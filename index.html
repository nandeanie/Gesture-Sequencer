🎵 What is this? (Explained simply!)

🎹
It's a music machine! Imagine a big grid of squares — like a tic-tac-toe board but much bigger. Each square is a musical note. When the line sweeps across and hits a lit-up square, it plays that note!
✋
Your hand controls it! Move your mouse (or your real hand on webcam!) over the grid. Wherever you hover, that square glows and gets "energy." The more you hover, the brighter it gets. Brighter = louder note!
🌊
It fades like water! When you move away, the energy slowly drains out — like a puddle drying up. Move your hand back and it fills up again!
🧮
There's clever math inside! The original had to correct for the camera being tilted — like how a photo looks different if your phone is crooked. It used 3D math (rotation matrices!) to fix it automatically.
